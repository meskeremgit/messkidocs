Meet Messki: Your AI Agent

At Messki, we take pride in our innovative AI agent, Messki, designed to enhance your experience by providing quick and accurate assistance. Here's what Messki can do for you:

Constantly Updated Knowledge Base

Messki is powered by a continuously updated knowledge base, ensuring that you receive the most relevant and up-to-date information.

Direct Assistance for Service-Related Queries

Whether you're a customer or a service provider, Messki is here to help you find the right answers and connect with the right people. Ask Messki anything related to services, and it will provide you with accurate information and recommendations.

Location-Specific Expertise

Messki is trained using data gathered from your chosen city, allowing it to provide location-specific advice and recommendations. This ensures that you receive information that is relevant and tailored to your needs.

Efficient Decision-Making

Messki helps you make informed decisions quickly and efficiently, saving you time and effort. No more endless searches—Messki provides you with the answers you need in a modern and convenient way.

Daily Query Credits

For now, users can ask Messki up to 3 questions daily. Your query credits will reset every 24 hours, giving you fresh opportunities to get the help you need.

Messki Plus: Coming Soon!

More query credits will be available with our upcoming Messki Plus subscription, which will be launched soon. Stay tuned for more details on how you can upgrade and enjoy additional benefits.

By integrating Messki into the Messki platform, we aim to provide you with an unparalleled user experience, making it easier than ever to find the services you need and connect with the right providers.
