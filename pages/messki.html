Welcome to Messki

Messki is a platform designed to connect customers with service providers effortlessly. Whether you’re looking for a specific service or want to offer your expertise, Messki makes it simple and efficient.

We Are Not a Middleman

At Messki, we believe in fostering direct and transparent connections between customers and service providers. Here's how we ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience for everyone involved:

  • No Middleman Fees - You only pay what the service provider charges. No hidden costs or extra fees. Your money goes directly to the person providing the service.
  • Direct Communication -
    • For Customers: Once you find the ideal service provider, you can contact them directly via call or text using the contact information provided on our platform.
    • For Service Providers: You receive inquiries and booking requests directly from customers through your preferred contact methods, ensuring clear and open communication.
  • Flexible Payment Methods - Service providers have the flexibility to accept payments through various methods, including cash or deposit, based on their preference. This allows for a seamless transaction process tailored to individual needs.
  • Empowered Customers - Customers have full control over the selection and communication with service providers. You can:
    • Browse through a comprehensive list of service providers.
    • Check their ratings, reviews, and profiles.
    • Choose the one that best fits your requirements and directly get in touch with them.
  • Independent Service Providers - Service providers have the autonomy to manage their own listings and bookings. You can:
    • List your services with detailed descriptions and pricing.
    • Update or remove listings as needed.
    • Accept bookings and payments directly from customers without any interference.
  • Transparent Feedback System - Our rating and review system ensures that both customers and service providers can share their experiences openly, promoting trust and quality within the community.
