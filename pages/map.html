Custom Map Feature: Easy Location Sharing

At Messki, we aim to make location communication as seamless as possible with our custom map feature. Here's how it works:

Built on Google My Maps

Our custom map is built on top of Google's My Maps, creating a familiar and user-friendly experience similar to Google Maps.

Street Numbers for Easy Navigation

Our map includes street numbers for each intersection, making it easy to communicate precise locations with service providers. This additional layer of detail enhances the location-sharing process.

Independent Link Access

  • Open Map: When you tap the "Open Map" button, Messki opens the map in your default internet browser.
  • Persistent Link: Once you have the link to our map, you can access it from any browser, not just within our app.
  • Accessibility: The map is accessible without our platform via the provided link, offering flexibility and convenience.

Constant Updates

We continuously update the map to ensure accuracy. Street numbers may change, so it's important to check for updates regularly.

Availability

City-Specific: If the map is not available in your chosen city, you may need to wait until the map becomes accessible for your area.
