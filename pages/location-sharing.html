Location Sharing: Convenient and Secure

At Messki, we've made it incredibly easy for users to share their location with service providers with just a click of a button. Here's how our location sharing feature works:

Available for Mobile App Users

This feature is exclusively available for 9Messki app users on mobile devices. It is not available on the web version.

Easy and Quick Location Sharing

  • Send Location: When you decide to share your location, simply tap the "Send Location" button within the app.
  • Permission: Accept the permission to access your location when prompted.
  • Auto-Fill Data: Messki will automatically retrieve your location and open your messaging app, pre-filling the necessary information.
  • Send Message: You can then send the location details to the service provider.

One-Time Location Sharing

  • Single Instance: This location sharing is a one-time action. It only shares your location at the moment you tap the "Send Location" button. It does not track your location continuously.
  • Privacy: Your privacy is our priority. Your location is only shared when you explicitly choose to do so.

Message Charges

Cost: Please note that sending location details will incur a charge for the message you send, based on your SIM card provider's message cost.

By offering a seamless and secure way to share your location, we aim to enhance the user experience and ensure smooth communication between customers and service providers.
